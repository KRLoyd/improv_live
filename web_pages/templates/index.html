Welcome to Improv.live!

Improvisation doesn't have to be hard.

Too often at improv jams or practices, more time is spent choosing a game instead of playing it. There are hundreds of different improv games and it can be overwhelming to choose one you want to play.

The purpose of this site is to make improv games accessible to all by providing instructions to various improv games and to make it easy to choose what to play by providing filters and a wheel.

The Games

Do you know the number of people playing a game, but not what you want to play? That's okay!

The Games page allows you to filter the games shown based on number of players.

The Wheel

Do you know the number of players in a game, but don't want to read many game descriptions?

Let the Wheel choose a game for you!

Alternatively, if you know the game you're playing, but need a prompt, simply choose the type of prompt you need and spin the wheel for a randomized one!
